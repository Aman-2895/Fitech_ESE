Vegetarian Diet Chart for Weight Loss

Upon waking Drink a glass of lemon water mixed with honey or coconut water
Breakfast Include eggs with brown bread toast, 1 banana and 1 cup of green tea. You can also eat idlis or masala/plain dosa on certain days.
Lunch 1 portion of brown rice with tofu + grilled tomatoes or vegetable soup + a green salad
or
2 chapatis with whole wheat + mixed vegetables + curd or yogurt
Evening snack Brown bread sandwich with low-fat cheese or baked beans + grilled mushrooms
or tea / coffee
Dinner Curry or dal + 2 chapatis + vegetable / paneer + salad
